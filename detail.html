Creating an auto mix
Let the Canvas cloud to build you a killer mix
10 mins
-
Beginner

Auto mixes are the quickest way to build a mix with guarenteed high quality result with just a few clicks. Track suggestions are based on hand crafted mix relationships between tracks. These can be many a varied an with powerfull suggestion filtering you can craft a perfect playlist mixed just how you like it.

1. Drag the first track to the canvas

Auto mixes are the quickest way to build a mix with guarenteed high quality result with just a few clicks. Track suggestions are based on hand crafted mix relationships between tracks. These can be many a varied an with powerfull suggestion filtering you can craft a perfect playlist mixed just how you like it.

Animated Gif
2. Select the track and choose a track to auto mix

With the track selected (on canvas) the control panel updates to show track options. Auto mix options fall under two categories; local and community mixes. Switch between the two via the tabs. Selecting a track loads it in the canvas mixed into the previous track with a unique recipe of user defined settings - whether your own or a member of the community

Animated Gif
3. Select the new track and repeat

Selecting the new track now provides related auto mixes to continue your set.

Animated Gif
4. Save your set

Once you finished building, save it you your sets ready to be published
